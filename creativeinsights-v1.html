    Ad Fatigue

    Fatigue shows as response (CTR, completion) falling while costs (CPC, CPV) rise — catching it early signals when to rotate or refresh assets before efficiency erodes.

    Ad Fatigue — All Assets Impressions (bars): delivery volume.
    CTR and CPC (lines): response quality and click price.
    Falling CTR with rising CPC at steady delivery = fatigue across the asset pool.
    All series derive from daily delivery data.
    Fatigue Status — All Assets One row per asset; trend deltas compare the last 14 days vs the prior 14 days,
    computed from daily delivery data. Status is rule-based:
    Fatigued = CTR and VW100 both down ≥20% · Watch = either down ≥8% · else Fresh.
    Sorted worst-first. Scales to any number of assets.
    Ad Fatigue — Video Assets Video Watch 100% (left axis): completion quality.
    CPV and CPCV (right axes): price per view / per completed view.
    Completion falling while view costs rise = video fatigue — users skip earlier on repeat exposure.
