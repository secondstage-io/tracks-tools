    Edition Mix

    What players buy and what each edition contributes — the mix sets the realized price per unit, and its drift over time shows whether premium editions hold up after the preorder wave.

    Edition MixUnits and revenue per edition. Realized gross sits slightly below list × units

    Edition Mix by CohortEdition shares per install week. The launch wave skews to Deluxe/Ultimate
    (preorder upgrades); the mix normalizes toward Standard afterwards — which is
    why net per unit declines for later cohorts.
