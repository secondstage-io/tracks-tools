    Retention & Cohorts

    How long players keep coming back after install — the curve benchmarks overall quality, the cohort matrix shows whether newer player groups retain better or worse than earlier ones, guiding content and update timing.

    Retention Curve (D0 → D30)Share of each cohort still opening the game N days after install,
    blended across all matured cohorts.
    Weekly CohortsEach row is one install week; columns show the share still active N days later.
    Dashes mark cohorts too young to be measured at that milestone — they fill in
    as the cohort matures, they are not zeros.
