    Reduction Guidance

    The levers available today to reduce the footprint on both sides — advertising delivery and gameplay energy use.

    Digital Advertising
    • Optimise for small file sizes
    • Choose images over video where the objective allows
    • Keep videos short
    • Better creative performance typically means less wasted energy and emissions
    Greater transparency from advertising platforms is needed to enable further control over these emissions.
    End Users (Gameplay)
    • Apply techniques that increase the energy efficiency of the game
    • Dynamically reduce render resolution and cap FPS during periods of inactivity
    • Apply stricter “power saving mode” defaults in more emission-intense geographies
    See the SGA energy efficiency guide for more techniques.
    Methodology: this dashboard applies the methodology from the Sustainable Games Standard for measuring and disclosing under the Greenhouse Gas Protocol — Scope 3.1 “Purchased Goods and Services” (digital advertising) and Scope 3.11 “Use of Sold Products” (gameplay on PC/console). Refer to the SGA website for details.
