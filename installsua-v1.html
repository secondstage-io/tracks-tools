    How install dates work here: paid installs and CPI are cohorted — attributed to the campaign date of the spend that earned them — so recent figures keep growing until the 30-day attribution window (TRACKS standard) completes. The Installs by Install Date chart below instead counts installs on the day they actually occurred; those may result from spend on earlier days, tracked within the same window.
