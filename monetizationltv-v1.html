    Revenue Over Time

    How net revenue accrues across the launch window — base-game revenue follows the install curve and decays, add-on revenue builds as the player base matures. The day-by-day detail is in the table below.

    Daily Net Revenue by Source Base-game revenue follows the install curve and decays after launch;
    add-on revenue builds slowly as the player base matures — the long-term
    shape of a premium title. The dashed line is the cumulative total.
    How revenue works here: figures are modelled net revenue — storefront-reported gross at realized prices, minus refunds, platform fees and taxes — linked to TRACKS attribution so channel-level return can be computed. Console storefronts report with a 2–5 day lag and refunds mature over the policy window: recent days will still move. Per-channel revenue follows the same 30-day cohort attribution as CPI.
    Daily Revenue DetailDay-by-day sales for the last 14 days of the period. Units are sold units before refunds;
    net applies refunds, fees and taxes. Click any column to sort.

    LTV & Cohorts

    How player value develops after install — the curve shows net revenue per install maturing as add-ons attach, the cohort table shows whether later install waves are worth more or less than earlier ones.

    LTV Curve (Net € per Install, D0 → D30)Cumulative net revenue per install by days since install, blended across matured
    cohorts. D0 is the edition purchase; the slow climb afterwards is add-on attach.
    LTV by Weekly CohortNet € per install at each milestone, per install week. Dashes mark cohorts too young
    to be measured at that milestone — they fill in as the cohort matures, they are not zeros.
    The launch cohort’s higher D0 reflects its Deluxe/Ultimate preorder skew.

    LTV vs CAC by Channel

    What an install costs versus what it is worth, per acquisition channel — the decision table for shifting budget toward channels that acquire valuable players, not just cheap installs.

    LTV vs CACCPI is cohorted (30-day attribution window); ARPI is modelled net revenue attributed
    to the channel’s installs. ROAS = ARPI ÷ CPI. Creator media is deal-booked — no honest
    CPI exists (see Influencer & Direct Buy); organic installs have no acquisition cost.
    Click any column to sort.
