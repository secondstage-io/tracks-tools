    Competitive Standing

    The full listening metric set side by side with competing titles — showing where the brand leads, where it trails, and which gaps are closing as the campaign runs.

    Brand Benchmark EMV totalled over the period; SoV averaged; mentions summed; followers/reach latest snapshot.
    Established franchises dominate non-social reach (news, wikis, web presence built over years) —
    a prelaunch title is social-led. The per-mention and share metrics are the fair comparison.

    Influencers & Health Indices

    Who carries the conversation and how the composite brand indices develop — identifying creators worth partnering with and whether brand equity is compounding over the flight.

    Top Influencers Profiles ranked by reach across their mentions of the game.
    Bots and auto-moderators are filtered out.
    Composite Health Scores SoV %, Buzz, Consideration and Awareness indices per week.
    Buzz = geometric mean of interaction/reach/mention shares. Consideration = geometric mean of
    affinity, wishlists, followers indices. Awareness = √(buzz × EMV share) × sentiment ratio.
